# Glicko Rating System Experiment

*Testing whether the Glicko-1 rating system (with uncertainty tracking via RD) improves on O/D Elo + Platt.*

## Method

Glicko-1 extends Elo with a Rating Deviation (RD) per team.
- **g(RD)** = 1 / sqrt(1 + 3q²RD²/π²) — scales prediction toward 0.5 when opponent RD is high
- High RD → conservative predictions (early season, QB change, new team)
- Between seasons: RD ← sqrt(RD² + c²) — uncertainty grows during offseason
- MOV: capped_linear, scale=0.05, cap=2.0 (same as incumbent)
